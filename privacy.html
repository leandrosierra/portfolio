Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data leandro-sierra.com and its product subdomains collect, how it is used, and the choices you have. It applies to the homepage and to every product hosted on a *.leandro-sierra.com subdomain.

Last updated: 14 June 2026.

Who is responsible

These sites are operated by Leandro Sierra, an independent developer (the "operator"). For any privacy question or request, contact leandrosierra1@gmail.com.

What we collect

  • Usage analytics. We measure aggregate traffic (pages viewed, referrer, approximate region, device type) using GoatCounter, a privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics tool. This data is aggregated and does not identify you.
  • Information you provide. If you email us or submit a form on a product, we receive what you send (for example your email address and message) and use it only to respond.
  • Technical logs. Our servers keep standard request logs (IP address, timestamp, user agent) for security and reliability. These are kept for a limited period and not used to profile you.

Cookies and advertising

Some products display advertising to fund the service. Where ads are shown, we use Google AdSense.

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this and other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the internet.
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can also opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalised advertising at aboutads.info/choices.
  • We use Google Consent Mode: advertising and analytics storage are set to "denied" by default until you accept via the consent banner. If you decline, non-personalised handling applies where required by law.

How we use data

We use the limited data above to operate and improve the products, understand which content is useful, respond to your messages, keep the services secure, and — where you have consented — show relevant advertising.

Legal basis (EEA/UK)

Where the GDPR or UK GDPR applies, our legal bases are: your consent (for advertising and any non-essential cookies), and our legitimate interests (cookieless analytics, security logging) balanced against your rights.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete or restrict processing of your personal data, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any right, email leandrosierra1@gmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only with the service providers needed to run the sites (hosting, the analytics provider, and Google for advertising where applicable), each acting under their own terms.

Children

These services are not directed to children under 16 and we do not knowingly collect their personal data.

Changes

We may update this policy as the services or applicable law change. The "last updated" date above reflects the current version.
